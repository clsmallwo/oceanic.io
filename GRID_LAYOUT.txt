BRIDGE LOCATIONS:
=================
Only 2 Bridges - Positioned in separate quadrants:

Bridge 1 (Northwest Quadrant):
  - Position: (7, 7)
  - Angle: 45° (diagonal \)
  - Crosses: Top-left to bottom-right diagonal
  - Cleared cells: (6,6), (7,7), (8,8)

Bridge 2 (Northeast Quadrant):
  - Position: (13, 7)
  - Angle: -45° (diagonal /)
  - Crosses: Top-right to bottom-left diagonal
  - Cleared cells: (12,8), (13,7), (14,6)

PLAYER STARTING POSITIONS:
==========================
  - Player 1 (Blue):  Grid (10, 2)  - Top Edge (North)
  - Player 2 (Red):   Grid (17, 10) - Right Edge (East)
  - Player 3 (Green): Grid (10, 17) - Bottom Edge (South)
  - Player 4 (Gold):  Grid (2, 10)  - Left Edge (West)

Note: Bases are positioned at the midpoints between the X arms,
      forming a cardinal cross pattern (N/S/E/W) around the center.

STRATEGIC NOTES:
================
1. The X-shaped trench divides the map into 4 quadrants
2. Each player starts at a cardinal direction (N/S/E/W)
3. Two bridges in separate quadrants provide crossing points
4. Bridge 1 (NW) and Bridge 2 (NE) are separate strategic objectives
5. Players must choose which bridge to contest
6. No single central crossing - creates asymmetric gameplay
7. Each player has different optimal routes to opponents
8. Control both bridges for full map mobility
9. Defensive units patrol around their home base
10. Offensive units must navigate to bridges to cross trenches

TURN SYSTEM:
============
- Each player gets 30 seconds per turn
- Deploy units during your turn
- Units move and attack automatically
- Click "END TURN" to pass to next player
- Turns auto-advance when timer expires
